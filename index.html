Privacy Policy – Professional Chess App

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Professional Chess! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Our chess app does not collect any personal information from you. We do not require registration, login, or any mechanism for tracking user data.

3. Usage Data

The app may store game moves temporarily in your browser session to allow undo/redo and move history. This data is not shared with anyone and is cleared when the browser session ends or the page is refreshed.

4. Cookies and Tracking

The app does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics.

5. Third-Party Content

The app may load chess piece images from external sources (for example Wikimedia Commons). We do not control these external sites and recommend reviewing their privacy policies if you have concerns.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your local session data. Since no personal data is collected, risks are minimal.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this policy text.
